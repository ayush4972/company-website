The Company

Built in Kathmandu,
for the public good.

Trasier Technology is a mission-driven engineering company building the digital infrastructure of modern civic life in Nepal.

We don't just build apps; we architect the operational systems that modern society runs on. From citizen-facing platforms to the intelligence layer beneath them, our work sits where technology meets everyday public life — and it has to work for everyone, on any device, on any network.

Based out of Kathmandu, our focus is local but our standards are global. We believe in high-density data, low-latency governance, and the quiet power of transparent, dependable engineering. We started with a single road complaint and set out to build the infrastructure layer for an entire valley — and beyond.

Small team, high standards, real deployments. That's the whole idea.
